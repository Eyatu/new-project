'MAYNKREFT HAUSE TUTORIYIL'

Helo gays tuday i wil be tiçing yu haw tu meyk a wuden hause.

IT IS AKCHUALY VERI SIMPIL. al yu nid is sam wud and sam mor wood. tugedir
will meyk a wuden hause end a wuden dor. yor dor vil pirotekt yu agenst monstirs et nayt. tenk yu!

  1. "First get da wuud firam a tiri."
  2. "Eftir yu get da wud turn it intu sam wudin pilenks. "
  3. "Wit dos wudin pilenks yu ken meyk a hause det şuld bi et list 5x4. Try to meyk da ruf 4 hay."
  4. "Den turin sam of dos pilenks intu 2 wudin dors. put dos dors ver yu vant da firant entrins."
  5. "Det shuld bi di end o yor hause. Hev a seyf nayt and be ver af kiripars. Dey ken blow yor hause."
